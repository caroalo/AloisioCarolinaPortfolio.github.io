Sales Report

This interactive sales dashboard provides a detailed view of sales performance over time.



Users can filter by month to observe monthly sales trends and explore key metrics such as:

  • Year-over-year sales growth (YoY%)
  • Total quantity sold
  • Average quantity sold per month

This tool allows for identifying sales patterns and making data-driven decisions based on historical data.
