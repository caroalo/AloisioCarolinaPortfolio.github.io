SALES AMOUNT PER PRODUCT CATEGORY

This interactive dashboard presents an analysis of sales by product category. It was constructed as a personal project.


Scatter graph:

It shows the amount of sold goods in relation to the cost of the goods sold. The data can be filter by year.

Bubble graph:

It shows the quantity of sold goods in relation to the average product price.

Bar char:

It shows how much was sold per product category, which can also be filtered using the squares on the right.
