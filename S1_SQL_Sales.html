4. Data Cleaning


a. Exploration of CLIENT TABLE:

As it can be observed, there are NULL values in the address field for some clients, and the balance is $ 0 for others.




b. Elimination of NULL Balance values:

Clients with a balance of $0 were deleted.




c. Update of NULL address values:

NULL values in the ‘address’ or ‘idLoc’ field were replace with ‘UnKknown’.








5. Queries

a. Client Listing: Retrieved IDs, names, addresses, city names, and state names of clients.






b. Seller Ranking: Ranked sellers based on the number of clients.






c. Locations Without Clients: Identified locations with no associated clients.






d. Top Seller by Client Count: Determined the seller with the highest number of clients.






e. Balance Sum by Seller: Calculated the total balance managed by each seller.



f. Clients with Balance Over 100: Listed clients with balances greater than 100, sorted in descending order.
