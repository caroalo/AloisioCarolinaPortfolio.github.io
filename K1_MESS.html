SUSTAINABLE PROCESS FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF MICROEMULSIONS CONTAINING PLANT ACTIVE SUBSTANCES

This project was presented at the InSudPharma Innovation Contest, showcasing a novel and sustainable process for developing microemulsions incorporating plant-derived active substances. The goal was to create an eco-friendly process and formulation that enhances the solubility and stability of these natural compounds. In addition, a patent application was filed to protect the innovative aspects of this process, reflecting its potential impact in the pharmaceutical industry.
